# 🏛 Empire Builder – Rise of Ancient Rome

A browser-based strategy game where you take control of Ancient Rome and rule as Emperor.  
Balance your **Military**, **Economy**, and **Happiness** to keep your empire alive.

Can you survive 10 turns without Rome collapsing?

---

## 🎮 Gameplay

Each turn, a random event appears.  
You must choose between two decisions.

Every choice affects:
- 🛡 Military
- 💰 Economy
- 😊 Happiness

If any stat falls to **0**, your empire falls.

Survive all turns to win!
